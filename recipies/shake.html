Maple PB Protien Shake

This maple peanut butter protien shake will have both your muscles and tastebuds SCREAMING for more!

To make this delicious shake, you will need the folowing ingredients and tools:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Maple Syrup
  • Protien Powder
  • Milk
  • Bananas
  • Chocolate Chips (optional)
  • A Blender
  • A cup

Now follow these simple steps:

  1. Combine the peanut butter, maple syrup, protien powder, milk and bananas in the blender.
  2. Blend on high until the mixture is smooth and consistnent.
  3. Pour the shake into a cup of your choice.
  4. Top with sliced bananas and chocolate chips. (optional)
  5. Enjoy!
